About Us

AshaKiran Jubilee Hope Center Hospital was inaugurated in 2004 by Bishop Valerian D'souza and is managed by Karunalaya Trust.

It has been duly registered with the Charity Commissioner Regd. No. (E-1612 Pune) and registered under Foreign Contribution (Regulation) Act, 1976, Regd. No. 083930465.

With the initial help of private donors and funding agencies like the Catholic Relief Services, Eichstatt Diocese (Germany), N. A. C. O., many deserving people suffering from various health problems have been treated at AshaKiran.

From 2013, the Trustees of Karunalaya Trust along with the panel of Doctors began working towards building AshaKiran Hospital, a multi-disciplinary healing centre. With the serene location, the ambiance of the building, available infrastructure, equipment, and dedicated doctors, medical and paramedical staff, we have Allopathic, Ayurvedic, Homeopathic medicine, counselling and all types of investigations and diagnostic services available.

Therefore, AshaKiran is now known as AshaKiran Hospital and Holistic Therapy Centre. People, no matter which caste or creed, whether professionals or non-professionals, healthy or sick, are all welcome to AshaKiran. We can join hands to serve and be served in AshaKiran Hospital (Holistic Therapy Centre).

Establishment

AshaKiran Jubilee Hope Centre, a 40 bedded hospital, was inaugurated in 2004 by Bishop Valerian D'Souza to provide medical care & support to the poor. It is a Non Profit making organization managed by Karunalaya Trust, a Medical Trust of the Diocese of Poona with Rt. Rev. Thomas Dabre, Bishop of Poona as the Chairman of the Trust. The Diocese of Poona extends to four districts of Western and Southern Maharashtra i.e. Pune, Sangli, Satara, Kolhapur city and Solapur district, admeasuring an area of 49,678 Sq. Kms.

As part of the outreach program, the medical team of Ashakiran regularly conducts free medical check-up camps and Blood Donation camps in Ashakiran Hospital, schools, institutions and localities.
